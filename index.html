Terms & Conditions

1. Participation Eligibility
Open to students aged 6–17 years. Participation is free of charge.

2. Student IP & Copyright
Students retain full copyright of their artwork. By submitting, participants grant Awareness by Art a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to display and share artwork for platform operations, CSR reporting, and educational purposes. Artwork will not be sold commercially without written consent.

3. DPDP Act Compliance
We comply fully with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. Student data is handled via teacher-mediated model. Minimal data is collected — only name, grade, school, and artwork.

4. Content Policy
All submissions are moderated for appropriate content. Submissions violating content guidelines will be disqualified. Zero tolerance for inappropriate imagery.

5. Prize Distribution
Prizes are awarded based on jury decisions which are final and binding. CSR surplus funds are transparently transferred to NGO plantation partners.

6. Environmental Commitment
All platform surplus funds after operations are transferred to SankalpTaru or SayTrees for verified tree plantation with blockchain tracking.
